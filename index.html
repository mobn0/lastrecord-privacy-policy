Privacy Policy for LastRecord

Last updated: June 7, 2026

LastRecord is designed to be simple and private. The app does not collect, sell, share, or track your personal data.

Data You Create

LastRecord lets you create records and save timestamps for when those records were completed. This information is stored on your device. If iCloud is enabled, it may also sync through your private iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit service.

iCloud Sync

When iCloud sync is used, your records are handled by Apple’s iCloud and CloudKit services. The developer of LastRecord does not have access to your records, timestamps, or private iCloud data.

No Tracking or Ads

LastRecord does not use advertising, analytics, third-party tracking, or third-party data sharing.

Deleting Data

You can delete records and timestamps inside the app. You can also remove the app from your device. iCloud sync and retention behavior may also depend on your Apple ID and iCloud settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer at noejadenmarx@gmail.com.
